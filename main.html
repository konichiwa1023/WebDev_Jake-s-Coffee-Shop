Menu

Coffee

Espresso

Espresso is a strong and concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a rich and intense flavor, with a layer of crema on top, and serves as the base for many coffee beverages.

Cappuccino

A traditional Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a creamy and frothy texture.

Latte

A popular espresso-based drink made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam, offering a creamy and smooth taste.

Mocha

A delightful combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream, perfect for those with a sweet tooth.

Americano

Americano is made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, resulting in a coffee that is similar in strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile. It has a smooth and milder taste compared to espresso.

Macchiato

Macchiato, meaning "stained" in Italian, is an espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam. It has a strong espresso flavor with a hint of milk, making it a bolder coffee choice.

Tea

Black Tea

Black tea is fully oxidized and has a stronger flavor compared to green tea. It is commonly enjoyed with milk and sugar and is known for its bold and robust taste. Black tea is also a good source of caffeine.

Green Tea

A light and refreshing tea made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, known for its grassy flavor and numerous health benefits.

Oolong Tea

Oolong tea is partially oxidized, resulting in a flavor profile that falls between green and black tea. It has a floral and fruity taste with a smooth finish. Oolong tea is often recommended for its metabolism-boosting properties.
